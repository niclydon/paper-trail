## Documentation

Append work to `CHANGES.md` (newest on top) as it happens. For migrations, incidents, rewrites, or multi-phase work, write a narrative at `docs/narrative/<YYYY-MM-DD>-<topic>.md` covering trigger, alternatives rejected, approach, verification, and what's unblocked.

See `DOCUMENTARY_STYLE_DOCUMENTATION-LITE.md` (imported via your top-level `CLAUDE.md`) for the standard.
